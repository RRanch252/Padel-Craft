Company Overview

MANZA SPORT, established in 2011 in Valencia, Spain, has grown to become one of the top four padel court manufacturers globally. The company operates a dedicated manufacturing facility in Valencia and maintains a financially robust position within the sports equipment industry.

With installations in over 70 countries and an average production capacity of 1,000 courts annually (as reported by distribution partners), MANZA SPORT has established a significant international presence in the padel court market.
