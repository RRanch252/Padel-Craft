Professional Playing Surfaces

The foundation of every great padel experience. Choose the surface that matches your players' needs and your facility's ambitions.

Your surface choice affects everything: ball speed and bounce, player comfort during extended play, maintenance requirements, and long-term durability. Here's how each option performs in real-world conditions.

Most Popular

Mondo STX Super Court

The choice of World Padel Tour. This monofilament surface delivers consistent ball bounce and superior player comfort, making it ideal for clubs attracting serious players.

  • Perfect for 4+ hours daily play
  • Maintains consistent speed in all weather
  • Preferred by professional players
  • Weekly brushing maintains performance
  • 5-7 year appearance retention
Best Value

Fibrillated Professional

Engineered for high-traffic facilities. Exceptional durability with lower maintenance requirements, perfect for busy clubs prioritizing reliability.

  • Handles 6+ hours daily use
  • 7 vibrant color options
  • Excellent drainage performance
  • Forgiving for beginners
  • Minimal maintenance needs
Premium Choice

Textured Elite

The pinnacle of playing surfaces. Crimped texture technology provides championship-level ball response and unmatched player satisfaction.

  • Tournament-grade performance
  • Superior grip in wet conditions
  • Natural clay court feel
  • Temperature stable year-round
  • 7-10 year premium warranty
