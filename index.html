About Padel-Craft

Padel-Craft is the official supplier of MANZA SPORT courts in Ireland, bringing world-class padel infrastructure to the Irish market. We are dedicated to supporting the growth of padel across Ireland through premium-quality court installations.

As the official supplier of MANZA SPORT courts, we provide comprehensive turnkey solutions from initial planning consultation through to installation, aftercare, and ongoing support. Our expertise ensures every project meets international standards while being tailored to local requirements.

We provide expert consultation throughout your project journey, including planning advice, design optimization, and full installation services. While most clients choose our complete turnkey service for peace of mind and single-source accountability, we also offer supply and installation services for those managing their own groundworks.

Common Questions About Our Services

Q: "We have our own groundworks contractor - can you work with them?"
A: "Absolutely. We'll provide detailed specifications and work closely with your team to ensure perfect preparation for your MANZA courts. Our technical team is available for consultation throughout the groundworks phase."
Q: "What if we start with supply-only but need more help?"
A: "We're flexible. Many clients who begin managing their own groundworks choose to hand over the complete project to us for peace of mind. We can step in at any stage to ensure project success."
Q: "How do we know which service option is best for us?"
A: "During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your project requirements, timeline, and resources to recommend the most suitable approach. Most clients find the turnkey solution offers the best value and peace of mind."
