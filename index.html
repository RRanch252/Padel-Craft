About Padel-Craft

Padel-Craft is the official supplier of MANZA SPORT courts in Ireland, bringing world-class padel infrastructure to the Irish market. We are dedicated to supporting the growth of padel across Ireland through premium-quality court installations.

As the official supplier of MANZA SPORT courts, we provide comprehensive turnkey solutions from initial planning and design through to installation, aftercare, and ongoing support. Our expertise ensures every project meets international standards while being tailored to local requirements.

We manage the entire process from planning to aftercare, providing a seamless experience for our clients. Whether you're establishing a new padel club, upgrading sports facilities, or installing private courts, we deliver complete project management solutions.

CURRENT PROJECT

Padel Society - Muckamore Cricket Club, Antrim

Project Overview

Padel-Craft is proud to be the exclusive installer for Padel Society's groundbreaking venture - Northern Ireland's premier padel facility. Selected through competitive tender, this multi-million pound investment represents a significant milestone in bringing world-class padel infrastructure to Northern Ireland.

Project Details:
  • 4 Panoramic Premium-Grade Outdoor Courts from MANZA SPORT
  • Opening: July 2025
  • Location: Muckamore Cricket Club, Antrim
  • Investment: Multi-million pound project
  • Order to Sign off time: under 30 days
  • Expansion Plans: Two additional sites in South Belfast in next 12 months
