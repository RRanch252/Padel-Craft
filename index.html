PADEL / CRAFT

Padel-Craft is the official supplier of MANZA SPORT padel courts in Ireland, providing world-class padel court infrastructure to clubs, sports facilities, and private clients nationwide. We specialise in turnkey solutions, managing the entire process from planning, design, supply, and installation through to aftercare and ongoing support.

We deliver MANZA SPORT's proven quality with projects across Europe, the US, and the Middle East.
