CURRENT PROJECT NORTHERN IRELAND

Padel Society - Muckamore Cricket Club, Antrim

Project Overview

Padel-Craft is proud to be the chosen installer for Padel Society's groundbreaking venture - Northern Ireland's premier padel facility. This multi-million pound investment represents a significant milestone in bringing world-class padel infrastructure to Northern Ireland.

Project Details:
  • 4 Panoramic Premium-Grade Outdoor Courts from MANZA SPORT
  • Opening: July 2025
  • Location: Muckamore Cricket Club, Antrim
  • Investment: Multi-million pound project
  • Future Plans: Two additional sites in South Belfast
